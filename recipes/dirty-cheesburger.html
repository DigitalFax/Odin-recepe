The Dirty Cheeseburger

This is a dual cheese, double beef patty, cheeseburger. This recipe wil teach you how to make this burger from start to finish, buns included. The entire recipe can be comleted anywhere you can build a fire as its entirly cooked on a coal fire, once again, including hte buns.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Gherkins
  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Blue Cheese
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Mayonaise
  • Tomato Puree
  • English Mustard
  • Wholegrain Mustard
  • Minced Beef
  • Pepper
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Oregano
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Make a fire.

    2. Now we prep the dough for the bun.

  2. Melt your butter.
  3. Get a bowl and add the following ingredients:
    • Flour
    • Salt
    • Sugar
    • Yeast
  4. Mix the ingredients.
  5. Add melted butter.
  6. Add one egg.
  7. Add warm water while stirring.
  8. Mix the ingredients well until doughy consistancy.
  9. Knead the dough.
  10. Sprinke flour on the dough and cover with a lid to rise.

    11. Now to prep the burger ingredients.

  11. Dice an onion into small to medium pieces.
  12. Dice a gherkin into small cubes.
  13. Cut lettuce into pieces, size doesn't matter. ;)
  14. Cut cheddar cheese into slices.
  15. Cut blue cheese into slices.

    16. Back to the bun.

  16. Knead the dough one last time while mixing in more flour.
  17. Shape the dough into a ball.
  18. Add the dough to a pre-heated lightly oiled pan.
  19. Smear the top of the dough with egg yolk to brown it while baking.
  20. Sprinke sesame seeds over the dough.
  21. Add a lid in the form of an upside down pan then cover the pan with hot coals.

    22. Now we will make a sauce.

  22. Get a mixing bowl and add the diced onions and pepper.
  23. chrush the onions and pepper mix into a paste.
  24. Add the following ingredients to the paste:
    • Mayonaise
    • Tomato Puree
    • English Mustard
    • Diced gherkins
    • Wholegrain Mustard
  25. Mix well.
  26. Remove the bun from the pot when it's done baking.

    27. Now we will prep the patties.

  27. Place the beef mince on your cutting board and add the following ingredients:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Chilli flakes
    • Oregano
  28. Mix well and split the mince into two patties.
  29. place the two patties in a pre-heated, oiled pan.
  30. Flip the patties when they are crispy on the pan side.
  31. Top each patty with cheese slices, one with blue cheese and the other with cheddar cheese
  32. Cover with lid untill patties are cooked and the cheese is melted.

    33. Now to assemble the burger.

  33. Cut the bun in half.
  34. Add sauce to the bottom bun.
  35. Add desired amount fo lettuce.
  36. Add sliced gherkins.
  37. Add the fisrt patty.
  38. Add the second patty.
  39. Add sauce to the top bun and cover the patties.
  40. Enjoy.
