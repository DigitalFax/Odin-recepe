Skillet Pizza

Amazing Skillet Pizza prepared in the Forest.

This is a cheese filled, chicken and pork pizza, that can be cooked in a pan anywhere you can build a fire.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Tomato pure
  • Mozzarella
  • Chilli
  • Smoked pork belly
  • Chicken breast

    • Steps

      Fisrt, we prepare the dough.

    1. Make a fire.
    2. Get a bowl and add your flour.
    3. Add yeast and salt.
    4. Add olive oil and warm water.
    5. Mix the ingredients.
    6. Add flour and keep mixing until a doughy consistency.
    7. Add olive oil and cover with a lid to rise.

      8. We will now prepare the pizza base sauce.

    8. Cut garlic into fine pieces.
    9. Cut tomato into small cubes.
    10. Cut Basil into small pieces.
    11. Cut an onion into small cubes.
    12. Add the following ingredients:
      • Salt
      • Pepper
      • Oregano
      • Olive oil
      • Tomato pure
    13. Mix all ingredients well.

      14. Now we will prepare the toppings.

    14. Cut your mozzarella into long strips.
    15. Cut a chilli into desired sized pieces.
    16. Cut your pre-smoked pork belly into cubes.
    17. Cut your chicken breast into cubes.
    18. Add the following ingredients to a pre-heated, oiled pan:
        Cut pork belly
    19. Cut Chicken breast
    20. Oregano
    21. Pepper
    22. Keep on heat and stir regularly until cooked.

      23. Time to prepare the base.

    23. Add flour to your working surface.
    24. Place your dough and add more flower.
    25. Knead the dough, then flatten into a circle slightly bigger than your pan.
    26. Place your mozzarella strips in a ring on the outside of the dough.
    27. Fold in the dough to cover the cheese and create a crust.
    28. Add the base to the pre-heated pan.
    29. Spread your base sauce over the base.
    30. Cover with mozzarella.
    31. Top with all previously prepared ingredients.
    32. Cover the pan with a lid and cover the lid with hot coals for even heat.
    33. Bake until brown and crispy.
    34. Serve and enjoy.
