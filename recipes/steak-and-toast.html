Steak and Toast

Steak with Chimichurri sauce, served with crunchy toasted bread

This is a garlic butter infused steak on home made toast topped with Chimichurri sauce. This recipe will cover the steak, bread and sauce and can be cooked on pot on a coal fire anywhere.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cummin
  • Chilli flakes
  • Warm water
  • Onions
  • Chilli
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Parsley
  • Oil
  • Oregano
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Thyme
  • Steak
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make a fire.

    2. First we will make a dough for the bread.

  2. Melt your butter.
  3. Get a bowl and add your flour
  4. Get a second bowl and add your yeast and warm water.
  5. Add the following ingredients to the flour:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Cummin
    • Chilli flakes
  6. Mix the flour and spice mix well.
  7. Add the yeast water while stirring.
  8. Add melted butter and continue mixing until doughy consistancy.
  9. Knead the dough.
  10. Cover dough to rise at put it aside.

    11. Next we will make the Chimichurri sauce.

  11. Dice an onion into small cubes.
  12. Cut open chillies and remove the seeds.
  13. Dice the chillies into small cubes.
  14. Dice garlic into small cubes.
  15. Dice fresh rosemary into small pieces
  16. Cut parsley into small pieces.
  17. Add the onions, chillies, garlic, rosemary and parsley into a bowl.
  18. Add olive oil the following ingredients:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Oregano
  19. Add red wine vinegar and mix well.

    20. Now back to the bread.

  20. Cover your working surface with flour.
  21. Knead dough once again.
  22. Shape the dough into a ball.
  23. Put the dough in a pre-heated lightly oiled pan.
  24. Cover with a lid and add hot coals on top for consistant baking.

    25. Now we will make garlic butter.

  25. Get a bowl and add the following ingredients:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Oregano
    • Garlic
  26. Crush the garlic and spice mix and bend well.
  27. Add unmelted butter.
  28. Mix all ingredients well and add the following:
    • Parsley
    • Rosemary
    • Thyme
  29. Mix well for one last time.
  30. From your garlic butter into balls for later and set aside.

    31. Now we will prepare the steak.

  31. Place your steak of choice on your working surface.
  32. Rub the steak with olive oil on both sides.
  33. Gererously season with salt and pepper.
  34. Remove your bread when it is done baking.
  35. Re-oil the pan.
  36. Sear all sides of the steak starting with the fat.
  37. Add most of your garlic butter to the pan.
  38. You can now cook your stake to desired doneness.
  39. Make sure to regularly cover the steak with the melted garlic butter.
  40. Remove your steak and place it on your working surface.
  41. Top it with your last garlic butter to melt.
  42. Cut your bread into slices.
  43. Toast the bread in the steak fat and oil left in the pan.

    44. We are now ready to dish.

  44. Place your toast on a plate or working surface.
  45. Cut your steak into slices.
  46. Place your steak on your toast and top with Chimichurri sauce
  47. Enjoy.
